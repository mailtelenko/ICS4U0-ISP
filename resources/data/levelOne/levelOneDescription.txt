Welcome to level one of Cyber Case! In this level, you will learn about the importance of mental health. On screen, you will see an individual whose name is Josh, and you are tasked to identify his mental health condition. By clicking on objects displayed on the screen, you will learn about the common characteristics and main causes of the mental health condition. Afterwards, you shall suggest solutions to deal with the issue, based on what you have learned. A quiz will conclude the level, and a 75% or greater is required in order to proceed to the next level. Otherwise, you may have to retry the level again. You will have 5 minutes to find 5 objects. Keep in mind that incorrect clicks will penalize your score. Good luck and have fun learning!
