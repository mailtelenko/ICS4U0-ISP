Welcome! This is level one of Cyber Case. In this level, you will learn about the importance of mental health. On screen, you will see an individual whose name is Josh. In this level, you are tasked to identify the Josh's mental health condition. By clicking on objects displayed on the screen, you will learn about the common characteristics and main causes of the mental health condition. Afterwards, you shall suggest solutions to deal with the issue, based on what you have learned. A quiz will conclude the level, and a 75% or greater is required in order to proceed to the next level. Otherwise, you may have to retry the level again.
