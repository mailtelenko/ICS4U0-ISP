Welcome! This is level two of Cyber Case. In this level, you will learn about the importance of physical health. On screen, you will see an individual whose name is Sam. In this level, you are tasked to identify Sam's physical condition. By clicking on objects displayed on the screen, you will learn about the common characteristics and main causes of the physical health conditions. Afterwards, you shall suggest solutions to deal with the issue, based on what you have learned. A quiz will conclude the level, and a 75% or greater is required in order to proceed to the next level. Otherwise, you may have to retry the level again.
