Welcome to the final level of Cyber Case! For your final task, you must use your detective skills to determine who is the suspicious individual messaging strangers online. You have 5 minutes to ask the users questions and close the chat window of the suspicious user. At the end of the level you will be presented with a quiz to based on what you learned during the level. A 75% or greater is required to pass the quiz, and any incorrect answers will negatively affect your final score. Good luck and have fun!
